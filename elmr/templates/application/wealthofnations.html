Visualization Controls

The Wealth of Nations visualization, inspired by Hans Rosling's 2006 TED Talk represents CPS data for 50 states in 5 dimensions:

  • X-Axis: size of the labor force
  • Y-Axis: the unemployment rate
  • Radius: the number employed
  • Color: economic region
  • Time: drag the slider to animate

Coming soon is the ability to change the dimensions of the chart
